## 1. Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: June 11, 2026**

Daadi ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

### 1.1 Information We Collect
- **Profile Information**: When you register, we collect your username and email address.
- **Match Data**: We collect information about your gameplay (moves, wins/losses) to provide statistics and multiplayer features.
- **Device Information**: We may collect technical details such as your device model and OS version to improve app performance.

### 1.2 How We Use Your Information
- To provide and maintain the App services.
- To facilitate real-time multiplayer matchmaking.
- To display global leaderboards and player statistics.
- To communicate system updates or administrative changes.

### 1.3 Data Storage & Security
We use Supabase for secure data storage. While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

### 1.4 Third-Party Services
We use PieSocket for real-time WebSocket communication and Google Play Services for app distribution. These services have their own privacy policies.

### 1.5 Your Rights
You may request the deletion of your account and personal data at any time through the App's settings or by contacting our support.
