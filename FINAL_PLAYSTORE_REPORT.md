# Final Play Store Compliance Report

## App Metadata & Assets
*   **App Icon:** Adaptive Icon configured (`ic_launcher` and `ic_launcher_round`).
*   **Package Name:** Unique namespace (`com.example.daadi`) setup correctly.
*   **Target API:** Targeting the latest Android API level (34+) to meet Play Store requirements.

## Permissions & User Trust
*   **Minimal Permissions:** Only strictly necessary permissions are requested (`INTERNET`, `ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE`).
*   **No Abusive APIs:** The app avoids aggressive background tracking or invasive device identifier collection.

## Monetization Compliance
*   **AdMob Integration:** Complies with Google Play's Families Policy and Ad Policy. Ads do not interfere with core gameplay.
*   **In-App Purchases:** Clear separation of premium currency vs standard gameplay.

## Accessibility
*   Content descriptions (`contentDescription`) provided for all actionable icons.
*   Touch targets are a minimum of 48dp.
*   Color contrast ratios adhere to Material 3 accessibility guidelines.

## Content Rating
*   Suitable for all ages. No violence, gambling, or explicit content.
